HOMOTEK Heavy-Duty Rubber Door Stops

Keep your doors safe, secure, and silent with our high-quality rubber door stops. Perfect for homes, offices, and any space where door control is essential.

Why Choose HOMOTEK Door Stops?

Our rubber door stoppers are engineered to provide maximum stability and an anti-slip base, ensuring doors close securely without slamming. The hollow design enhances friction, offering better grip on all floor types.

Each door stop comes with a self-adhesive holder, making installation and repositioning a breeze. You can easily adjust them as needed, and they stay firmly in place without damaging your floor.

Key Features

  • Anti-slip base for enhanced grip on all floor types
  • Self-adhesive holders for easy installation and repositioning
  • Hollow design increases stability and friction
  • Noise-reducing TPR material ensures quiet operation
  • Strong construction handles heavy doors safely

These door chucks are ideal for use in homes, offices, and other environments where door safety and control are a priority. They help prevent door damage, protect walls, and avoid lock-outs, offering reliable performance every day.

Versatile and Durable

Designed to fit under doors up to 1 inch off the floor, these rubber door stoppers are a versatile solution for any door type. The included adhesive hooks also make it easy to hang and store them when not in use, helping to keep your workspace safe and organized.

With their robust structure and quiet operation, HOMOTEK door stops offer both practicality and peace of mind. They are the perfect addition to any home or office looking for reliable door control without the hassle.
